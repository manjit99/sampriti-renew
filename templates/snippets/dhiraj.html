Dr. Dhiraj Patar, a man of many talents, has been active on the field of Literature and Journalism for about seven years now.

Specialization:

  • Early History of Assamese Literature.
  • Modern Assamese Literature.
  • Folklore and Cultural studies.

Work Profile:

  • Acting Chief Editor of Tiwa monthly newspaper, PATHRA.
  • Editor of quarterly literary magazine, RODALI.
  • Associated to the compilation of Famous Assamese Dictionary, HAMKOSHA.
  • Former Executive Editor of a monthly magazine called NINAD.
  • Former R.J of Radio Luit.

Published Books:

  • Assamese Poetry- Kiman Maram Karile Tumi Muk Bhal Paba.
  • Assamese Poetry- Bhalpowar Amuthi Xoish.
  • Tiwa Loka Sanskritir Abhash.
  • Assamiya Sahiyatar Bisar aru Bisleshan(edited).
